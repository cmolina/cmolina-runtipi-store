BentoPDF is a privacy-first, client-side PDF toolkit that lets you manipulate PDF files entirely in your browser. No files are ever uploaded to a server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

## Features

- 100+ PDF tools: merge, split, edit, convert, compress, encrypt, sign, OCR, watermark, and more
- All processing happens client-side in your browser - files never leave your device
- Convert to/from PDF: images, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Markdown, EPUB, and many more formats
- Built-in PDF editor with annotations, highlighting, redaction, and form filling
- Digital signature support with X.509 certificates
- No usage limits or file size restrictions
- Multi-language support with 18+ translations
